ABOUT
Felonius Gru (or simply Gru) (born sometime in 1960), is the son of Marlena Gru and the late Robert Gru, grandson of Madeleine Gru and Henry Gru, and is the main protagonist of the Despicable Me franchise. He is a former supervillain and is the husband of AVL agent Lucy Wilde. He has three adopted daughters Margo, Edith, and Agnes. Gru is also the leader of the Minions, is friends with Dr. Nefario and is also the twin brother of Dru Gru.
